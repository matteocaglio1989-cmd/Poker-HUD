# Privacy Policy — PokerEye HUD

*Last updated: April 13, 2026*

## Overview

PokerEye HUD ("the App") is a macOS poker heads-up display developed by Matteo Caglio. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how the App handles your data.

## Data Collection

PokerEye HUD does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing happens locally on your Mac.

Specifically, the App:

- Does not collect personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- Does not track your location
- Does not use analytics or tracking frameworks
- Does not send any data to external servers
- Does not share data with third parties
- Does not use cookies or similar technologies

## Hand History Data

The App reads poker hand history files stored locally on your Mac to generate statistics and display HUD overlays. These files are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded, copied, or transmitted anywhere.

## Subscriptions and Payments

PokerEye HUD offers optional in-app subscriptions managed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Payment information is handled by Apple and is never accessible to the developer. For details on how Apple handles your payment data, please refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/).

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

## Data Security

Since no user data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data breach from the App. All hand history processing and statistics remain on your device.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 17. Due to poker-related content, the App is rated 17+ on the App Store.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
